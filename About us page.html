About HealthPal Medical and Dental Supplies

Get to know our story and our commitment to providing quality medical and dental supplies.

Our Mission

We strive to be the leading provider of medical and dental supplies, offering a wide range of products to meet the needs of healthcare professionals and individuals alike.

Our Vision

Our vision is to improve access to essential medical and dental supplies, contributing to better healthcare outcomes for communities worldwide.

Our History

Healthpal was launched during the 2020 pandemic. We started as a distributor for Alcohol, Hand Soaps, Sanitizers and disinfectants. But as the time went by, it grew and began to cater other medical products, as well such as RF thermometers, pulse oximeters, oxygen and many other medical products. We also ventured into dental supplies.
